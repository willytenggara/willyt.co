A short testimony before the departure of the mission trip.

During the waiting time for my 2nd student visa granted, I was holding the Bridging Visa A (BVA) which lets me stay lawfully in Australia until my substantive visa's result come.
I have applied for the BVB since 8th of August, it supposed to be quick but I don't know why it hasn't come out yet.

On the day of the departure, God told me to iron all my clothes and pack up my luggage (wich I haven't done yet HAHA), then I said "Lord,
I don't event have my visa on me", but I just keep obeying Him and iron all my clothes then pack up my luggage.
